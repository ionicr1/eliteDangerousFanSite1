Player ships engaging a "Thargoid". The Thargoids are an alien race in Elite Dangerous.
They are quite rare to come across...
Player vs player combat, or PVP, is supported.
The majority of a player's time will be spent behind their ship's dash.
Customizables are available, such as bobbleheads.
When a ship begins to run low on fuel, a player is able to enter the vicinity
of a large star to "scoop" more fuel.
Elite Dangerous boasts a 1:1 scale of our current universe. This means our
current galaxy is able to be explored.
